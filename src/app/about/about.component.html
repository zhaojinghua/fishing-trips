About Fish Emeryville

We specialize in providing fishing adventures in San Francisco Bay and outside the Golden Gate. We feature nine of the finest sport fishing boats in Northern California, all fully equipped to make your day enjoyable. Each boat features the latest and most advanced fish finding and navigation equipment available. We focus on making sure our anglers have the trip of a lifetime pursuing the wide range game fish that inhabit San Francisco Bay, the Bay Area coastline, and Gulf of the Farallon Islands. All boats and Captains meet U.S. Coast Guard standards and are certified for your safety. All boats have comfortable inside seating with tables and clean restrooms. Our Captains specialize in putting anglers on King Salmon, California Halibut, Striped Bass, Rockfish, Lingcod, and Dungeness Crab utilizing the most effective methods for locating and catching fish. Whether it's Live Bait drifting, trolling or mooching, all of our Captains are focused on getting you your limit! You can be assured you are fishing with the most knowledgeable fleet in the San Francisco Bay Area with the goal of making your trip a rewarding and safe adventure. Private Boat Charters and Special Events All of our boats offer a wide variety of charter options, customized to meet your needs. Whether it's a small, private occasion, or a larger Company recognition event, we can design a trip to fit most requests. Trip possibilities include: • Corporate Events and Recognition trips-for employee's or outside business relationships • Whale Watching and Pelagic Bird Observation • Weddings, Anniversaries and Family parties • Fleet Week and Fireworks • Historical Bay Tours • Photo Opportunities and Kayak shuttle • Open Water Swim support and Triathlon's • Church, School or youth group outings. Some attractions you may encounter on your trip: Alcatraz Island, Golden Gate Bridge, Treasure Island, Fisherman's Wharf, AT&T Park, SF-Oakland Bay Bridge, Angel Island, Alameda Naval Air Base, Pt. Bonita lighthouse, Pier 39 and many, many more.
